Why EvoDoc now?

Health data is scattered and time is tight. EvoDoc condenses signals from symptoms, structured records, and context to suggest clinically-plausible differentials. You stay in control—EvoDoc reduces cognitive load.

  • Structured symptom intake with guardrails
  • Model suggestions + transparent rationale
  • Audit trail and versioning
  • Designed for clinician oversight
