Why EvoDoc now?

Health data is scattered and time is tight. EvoDoc condenses signals from symptoms, structured records and context to suggest **clinically-plausible** differentials and next steps. You stay in control - EvoDoc simply reduces cognitive load and surfaces nuances most systems miss.

  • Structured symptom intake with guardrails
  • Model suggestions + transparent rationale
  • Audit trail and versioning
  • Designed for clinician oversight - not autopilot
